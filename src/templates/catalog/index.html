Electrofusion Fittings

Electrofusion is a welding method used for pipe connections. In this method, the pipe ends are melted and joined by heating with electric current. Electrofusion has many advantages, for example it provides a high-quality and durable connection. Additionally, it can be applied and sealed quickly and easily.

Electrofusion is generally the preferred welding method for polyethylene pipes. This method is frequently used in piping systems used for water and gas transmission. Electrofusion welding machines are designed to suit pipes of different diameters and can be easily used by operators.

In general, electrofusion is the preferred method to obtain reliable and quality results in pipe connections. With the use of this method, the durability and longevity of piping systems can be increased.
